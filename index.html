The Scribe

Orion R. Veil

Orion R. Veil writes at the volatile intersection of alternative archaeology, forgotten human histories, and cosmic thrillers. Drawing creative atmospheric cues from comparative religion, hidden traditions, and the anomalous dark corners of modern reality, Veil’s works function as an open invitation to question where standard myth ends and pure ancestral memory begins.

Born within Muskogee, Oklahoma, and currently operating out of Texas, Orion frames stories around normal individuals caught inside grand, pre-engineered fates. Rather than serving comforting, convenient answers, his novels explore what occurs when ordinary people are forced to embrace what they were fundamentally bred to become.
